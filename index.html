About Kinetik Wind Power

A trusted provider of end-to-end solutions for the wind energy sector.

Led by K. Ramachandran, who brings over 25 years of industry experience, Kinetik Wind Power specializes in wind turbine installation, operation, and maintenance. We focus on innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction across various capacities.

Backed by a skilled team and industry-leading practices, we help clients maximize the performance and lifespan of their wind energy assets. Our commitment is to deliver sustainable energy solutions that drive efficiency and long-term value.

Our Core Services

Comprehensive wind energy solutions from installation to maintenance — keeping your turbines running at peak performance.

🔌

Electronics Module Service

Expertise in electronics module service, testing and replacement to ensure electrical safety and accuracy.

🔄

Panel Rewiring

Upgrading and reorganizing electrical panels for improved efficiency and safety compliance.

🔗

Cable Joint (up to 33kV)

Connecting, repairing and terminating low to high-voltage cables using heat-shrink and resin-filled kits for electrical continuity.

📦

Spare Parts Supply

Sourcing and supplying quality turbine components to minimize downtime and maintain operational readiness.

⚙️

Hydraulic & Yaw Unit Servicing

On-site hydraulic and yaw unit servicing and testing for optimal turbine directional control.

🔋

Generator Services

Generator cooling fan and bearing replacement, plus alignment work performed at nacelle top.

🏭

Gearbox Service

High speed shaft and bearing replacement, gear pump and 'V' ring replacement at nacelle top.

🔥

Nacelle & Panel Refurbishment

Comprehensive repair and restoration of fire-damaged nacelles and panels to restore operational capability.

🏗️

Installation & Commissioning

Full turbine installation, erection and commissioning services — expertise in Vestas RRB (225 kW, 500 kW, 600 kW).
